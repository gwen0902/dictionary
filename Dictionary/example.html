PENGERTIANVERB

Verb adalah kata kerja, Verb merupakan bagian terpenting dan selalu muncul dalam setiap kalimat. Kata kerja dari Bahasa Inggris ini juga sedikit unik.Kata kerja dalam Bahasa Inggris bisa mendeskripsikan sebuah kondisi. Contoh:

    Play

  • Verb 1: Eva Play every sunday
  • Verb 2: Eva played tennis last sunday
  • Verb 3: Eva have played tennis since junior high school

    • Buy

  • Verb 1: I buy food every day
  • Verb 2: I bought my mum a jewelry
  • Verb 3: We have boughta present for you

    • Eat

  • Verb 1: I eat one apple every day
  • Verb 2: i ate one apple yesterday
  • Verb 3: I have eaten one apple today

    • Awake

  • Verb 1: He will awake the snake that sleeps in the nest.
  • Verb 2: His sound awoke me from my sleep
  • Verb 3: She had been awekening up from her long sleep by my mother.

    • Watch

  • Verb 1: Ando watch movie in cinema almost every day.
  • Verb 2: Ando watched the movie last week.
  • Verb 3: Ando have watched all the movies.
